Nobre Cottage

The charming Nobre Cottage is a villa in Altura, just 3 minutes walking distance to the beach. In Nobre Cottage, our guests can enjoy a unique experience of what Altura and Algarve have to offer. In our installations, our guests can make use of our 2 exterior gardens, and a sunbathing rooftop.

The villa has a fully equipped kitchen, a living room with a satellite flatscreen and a fireplace, and two bedrooms. The house includes free wi-fi, Air Conditioning in rooms and living room, and private parking spot on location.

You can play tennis and golf at the property’s surrounding areas, and the area is popular for biking and hiking. Faro is 41.8 km from Nobre Cottage, and Huelva is 49.9 km from the property. The nearest airport is Faro Airport, 45.1 km from the property.
